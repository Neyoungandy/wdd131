FitPro Wellness

This name was chosen to reflect the focus on fitness and wellness for busy professionals. It is simple and easy to remember, which aligns with the target audience's need for quick access to fitness solutions.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the FitPro Wellness website is to provide quick, effective workout routines, meal planning tips, and stress management techniques for busy professionals who have limited time to invest in their health but want to maintain wellness.

Scenarios

  • What are some quick workouts I can do in under 20 minutes?
  • How can I plan healthy meals with limited time for cooking?

Color Schema

Primary Color: #28a745 (Green) – used for headings and buttons.

Secondary Color: #f8f9fa (Light Gray) – used for background and body text.

Typography

Heading Font: Montserrat (applied to all headings).

Body Font: Open Sans (applied to body text and paragraphs).
